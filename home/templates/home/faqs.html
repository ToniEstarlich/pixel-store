Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How can I return a product?

You can return any item within 30 days of purchase. Please ensure the product is in its original condition with all tags attached. Contact our customer service for further instructions.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping usually takes 3-5 business days. Expedited shipping options are available at checkout.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and PayPal.

How do I contact customer support?

You can contact us via email at support@pixelclothingshop.com or call us at +44 123 456 789.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we offer international shipping. Delivery times and fees will vary depending on your location.
